## Professional Heat Pump Installation Services

Future-proof your home with renewable heating technology. Our certified team installs ground source and air source heat pumps, delivering sustainable, efficient heating that reduces your carbon footprint and energy bills.

### Heat Pump Solutions We Offer

**Ground Source Heat Pumps**
Harness stable underground temperatures for highly efficient heating:

- Horizontal ground loops (larger plots)
- Vertical boreholes (compact installations)
- Slinky coil systems
- Pond and lake loops
- Complete system design and installation

**Air Source Heat Pumps**
Extract heat from the air for efficient year-round heating:

- External air-to-water systems
- Integration with existing heating systems
- Hot water cylinder installation
- Weather compensation controls
- Quiet operation systems

### Complete Service Package

**Suitability Assessment**
Not every property is suitable for heat pumps. We provide honest, expert advice:

- Property heat loss calculations
- Insulation and efficiency review
- System sizing and design
- Cost-benefit analysis
- Grant and incentive guidance

**Professional Installation**
MCS certified installations to the highest standards:

- Ground loop or external unit installation
- Internal pipework and controls
- Buffer tank and hot water cylinder
- Commissioning and testing
- Building control certification
- MCS certification for grants

**Integration with Existing Systems**
Heat pumps work best as part of a complete heating strategy:

- Underfloor heating integration
- Radiator upgrades (larger, low-temperature)
- Solar thermal compatibility
- Smart controls and monitoring
- Hybrid systems (heat pump + backup)

### Why Choose Heat Pumps?

**Environmental Benefits**
- Renewable energy source
- Significantly reduced carbon emissions
- No fossil fuels burned on-site
- Part of net-zero housing strategy

**Financial Advantages**
- Lower running costs than oil, LPG, or electric heating
- Eligible for Renewable Heat Incentive (RHI)
- Boiler Upgrade Scheme grants available
- Increases property value
- Qualifies for reduced VAT rates (5%)

**Performance Benefits**
- Highly efficient (300-400% efficiency)
- Consistent, controllable heating
- Cooling capability in summer (some models)
- Low maintenance requirements
- 20-25 year lifespan
