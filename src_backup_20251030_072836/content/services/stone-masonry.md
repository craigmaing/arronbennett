## Master Stone Masonry Services

Traditional stone masonry is at the heart of what we do. With over 20 years of experience working with Cornish stone and granite, we bring ancient skills to every project - from new builds to heritage restoration.

### Our Stone Masonry Services

**Stone Cladding**
Transform timber-frame buildings with authentic stone cladding:

- Traditional Cornish stone facing
- Granite and limestone cladding
- Weather-resistant construction
- Breathable wall systems
- Sympathetic to local architecture

**Chimneys**
Expert chimney construction and restoration:

- New chimney builds
- Stack restoration and repointing
- Flue lining and installation
- Historic chimney repair
- Granite and stone chimneys

**Walls and Boundaries**
Create lasting structures that define your property:

- Boundary walls (mortared and dry stone)
- Garden walls and features
- Retaining walls
- Cornish hedging
- Granite posts and pillars

### Traditional Cornish Techniques

**Dry Stone Walling**
Master craftsmanship requiring no mortar - just skill, experience, and carefully selected stones:

- Traditional Cornish hedging
- Agricultural and garden walls
- Heritage restoration
- Wildlife-friendly construction
- Structures that last generations

**Granite Hedging**
The iconic Cornish boundary:

- Authentic granite hedging
- Traditional construction methods
- Planted hedging (topsoil and vegetation)
- Restoration of collapsed sections
- New hedging for property boundaries

### Specialist Stonework

Our stone masonry extends to:

- **Feature Stonework** - Arches, lintels, quoins
- **Stone Steps** - External and internal staircases
- **Fireplace Surrounds** - Traditional and contemporary
- **Stone Restoration** - Cleaning, repointing, repair
- **Conservation Work** - Listed buildings and heritage sites

While we pride ourselves on traditional craftsmanship, all our stonework meets modern building regulations and standards. We combine centuries-old techniques with contemporary engineering knowledge to create structures that are both beautiful and technically sound.
