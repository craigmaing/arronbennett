## Expert Full Build Services in Cornwall & Devon

Whether you're dreaming of a bespoke new-build property or transforming a historic barn into your perfect home, Arron Bennett Building brings over 20 years of expertise to every project.

### Our Approach to Full Builds

We specialize in creating unique properties that blend traditional Cornish craftsmanship with modern building techniques. Every full build project receives:

- **Collaborative Design Process** - Working closely with you, your architect, and building control
- **Traditional Methods** - Stone masonry, lime works, and heritage building techniques
- **Modern Innovation** - Energy-efficient systems, sustainable materials, contemporary design
- **Complete Project Management** - One point of contact from groundworks to finishing touches

### Types of Full Build Projects

**New Build Properties**
Custom-designed homes built to your exact specifications, using sustainable materials and energy-efficient construction methods.

**Barn Conversions**
Sympathetic restoration of agricultural buildings, preserving character while creating comfortable modern living spaces.

**Heritage Buildings**
Specialist work on listed buildings and conservation areas, maintaining architectural integrity while meeting modern standards.

### Why Choose Us for Your Full Build?

With extensive experience in both traditional and contemporary construction, we understand the unique challenges of building in Cornwall and Devon. Our team works seamlessly with architects, engineers, and building control to ensure your project runs smoothly from concept to completion.

**Quality Guaranteed** - Every project backed by full insurance and 20+ years of proven craftsmanship.
