# House Extensions

Transform your home with a professionally designed and built house extension. We specialize in all types of extensions, from single-storey rear extensions to complex multi-storey projects.

## Our Extension Services

### Single Storey Extensions
Perfect for expanding ground floor living space, our single storey extensions blend seamlessly with your existing property.

### Double Storey Extensions
Maximize your space with a double storey extension, adding rooms both upstairs and downstairs.

### Side Return Extensions
Popular in terraced and semi-detached properties, side returns make excellent use of underutilized space.

### Rear Extensions
Open up your home with a rear extension, creating the perfect space for a kitchen-diner or family room.
